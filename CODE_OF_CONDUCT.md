# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the plutonium_engine community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Give and receive technical feedback with care.
- Prefer specific evidence over personal criticism.
- Help keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption
- sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- insults, slurs, demeaning comments, or personal attacks
- publishing private information without explicit permission
- conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently ban participants for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

Reports can be sent to `coding@zeke13.com`. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when safe to do so. Maintainers will handle reports privately and will not knowingly disclose reporter information beyond what is necessary to investigate and respond.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, community chat, and any public representation of the project.
